Quality Standards

Quality Assurance

Multi-Point Testing Routine

Before dispatching hardware, our crew runs strict diagnostics. Audio arrays are sweep-tested for speaker coil performance, LED tiles are calibrated for colour consistency, and lighting rigs undergo full pan/tilt cycle tests.

Redundancy & Backups

We employ a "zero-point-of-failure" production philosophy. Critical audio signals run on redundant Dante networks backed by analog lines, and power grids use parallel backup generators with automatic transfer switches.
