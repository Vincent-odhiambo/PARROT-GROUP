A Legacy of Technical Excellence

Parrot Group (founded as Parrot Concepts Limited in 2014) was born from a desire to move beyond basic canvas tents and low-grade PA speakers. Over the last decade, we have scaled our production warehouses in Nairobi to house standard scaffolding, intelligent lights, and linear acoustic arrays.

We believe event management is a blend of physics and brand psychology. Our riggers calculate wind-loads on truss towers, our audio technicians run system frequency calculations using acoustic models, and our designers draft 3D visual environments to ensure every attendee experiences flawless sightlines.

Whether launching telecom services in national stadiums, staging awards gala nights, or orchestrating wide-area outdoor activations, we provide full end-to-end technical production guarantees.
