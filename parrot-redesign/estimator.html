Scaling by Guest Volume

As guest count grows, audio projection and structural rigging requirements increase. For groups over 500, we transition from point-source speakers to hanging line source arrays. Large capacities trigger backup generators (150kVA+) and additional technical crew.

The Multi-Day Advantage

Event production has significant upfront logistics — transporting heavy trusses, assembling LED screens, rigging cables. Since mobilization occurs on Day 1, subsequent days are billed at a discounted rate (typically 45% of base daily cost).
